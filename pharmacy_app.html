🕓 Time Dependency
Level 2 temporal tracking — who changed what and when
This database uses Level 2 Time Dependency. Tables have created_at, updated_at, and updated_by columns. When you dispense a prescription, updated_at and updated_by are set automatically by the SQL UPDATE statement.
